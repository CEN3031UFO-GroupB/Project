Priorities and Satisfactions Categories

  • Family - immediate and/or extended relationship
  • Health - work out schedule, healthy diet
  • Rest and Relaxation - personal time to do something you enjoy
  • Faith - meditation, devotionals, meetings
  • Finance - plans for saving, investing, and managing
  • Romance - spending quality time with significant other
  • Friends - reaching out and connecting with people you care about
  • Contribution - giving of your time, talents, and treasures
  • Personal Growth - exposure to new things/people, seminars, books
  • Career - position provides growth, purpose, and financial stability
  • Physical Environment - personal space: car, home, neighborhood, city
